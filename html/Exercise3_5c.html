Filipino Macaroni Salad

Recipe & Cooking Instruction

-Filipino Sweet Macaroni Salad is a popular version of mac salad in the Philippines.
It is composed of sweetened fruits, condensed milk, and mayonnaise. Cheese is an
unconventional ingredient added into the mix. For many the idea of combining Macaroni,
Condensed Milk, Mayonnaise, Palm Fruit, Fruit Cocktail and Cheese is quite unusual to say
the least but for Filipinos it sounds like a great dessert and we call it Sweet Macaroni Salad.
This salad is very popular during the Christmas season and it is a version of the fruit salad
for the budget conscious, as fruit cocktail back home is quite an expensive commodity, it is
then extended with macaroni pasta to feed more.

Ingredients

  • 250 g elbow macaroni, undercooked by 1 minute
  • 400 g can fruit cocktail in heavy syrup, drained
  • 225 g can pineapple chunks, drained
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 340 g bottle green kaong, drained
  • 340 g bottle red nata de coco, drained
  • 1/2 cup cubed cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 400 g can condensed milk
